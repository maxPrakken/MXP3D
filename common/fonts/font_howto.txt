to make a font

step one:
	open CBFG, and make a font with a 512X512 resolution
	
step two:
	make sure character start is at 0
	
step three:
	export as bmp
	
step four: 
	go into the compresonator and import the .bmp
	
step five:
	give it the maximum available mipmaps (10 preferably)
	
step six:
	export it as a DDS DXT3 with adaptive checked
	
step seven: 
	enjoy your font
